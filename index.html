Scrabble Instructions and Rules

Welcome to Scrabble! Create words using the seven tiles given below. Your total score will be given when you click "Next Word." If you want to restart the game and reset the score, click "Restart."

As a rule, you must place letter tiles on the board squares only. If they are placed elsewhere, they will revert back to the tile rack.

Also, you must place letter tiles next to each other with no spaces in between. If there is a space between two letters, the letter tile cannot be dropped onto the square.

The Double Word Score squares double the total score of the word! If you place a letter on one of these squares, your score will be doubled. If you place a letter square on each, your word score will be quadrupled!

The score for each letter is shown on their tile.

Have fun playing!
